I’m Tracy Lilley — a lived-experience advocate, strategic navigator, and independent social worker.

I bring over a decade of community support, personal system navigation, and professional training together to help people be heard — especially when services are fragmented or dismissive.

After 40+ years of masking and a late ADHD diagnosis, I’ve seen how easily people fall through the cracks. That’s why I created this space — to help others find their voice and their pathway through the noise.
