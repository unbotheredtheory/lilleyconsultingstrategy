Real Stories. Real Change.

These are the words of people who chose to be heard, supported, and respected. Shared with their permission, their identities are protected.

I really don’t think I would be here if it were not for Tracy.

We knew each other at school and lost contact until sometime in the 2010s. This was when we started chatting via social media platforms, and I realised she had grown personally a lot. I was taken aback...

In 2021, I underwent several surgeries... Tracy reached out when I was at my lowest — drinking, prescription drugs, limited mobility, and a mental health spiral. She just knew.

It was Tracy who found a place for me to reside and get help. It was Tracy who told the health professionals what for, and Tracy who has kept me pushing for answers — because she heard.

Since then, she’s had my back completely. She listens, distracts with kindness (“Send me photos of your garden”), and gently helps me reset...

If Tracy hadn’t reached out, I believe I would have been dismissed until I took my own life. I wasn’t far from it.

I can’t thank her enough. I am privileged to call her a true friend, a lovely human, and an absolutely courageous wahine.

— Shared with permission, identity withheld for privacy
